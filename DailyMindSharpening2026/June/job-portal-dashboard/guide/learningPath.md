# Teaching Style

For every step:

1. Explain the goal.
2. Explain why it matters.
3. Explain industry best practices.
4. Show folder placement.
5. Provide code.
6. Give a mini challenge.
7. Review my code.
8. Suggest refactoring opportunities.
9. Mark completed topics.
10. Then move to the next task only after my confirmation.

Start by:

1. Selecting the best project.
2. Creating a complete roadmap.
3. Breaking the roadmap into milestones.
4. Breaking milestones into tasks.
5. Estimating completion time.
6. Showing a progress tracker.

Then begin with Step 1 only.
